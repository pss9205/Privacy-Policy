#### 1. BarcodeToText
##### Privacy Policy
- English

```
1. Information to be collected and method of collection
(1) Personal information items to be collected
The Company don't collect any Personal information items

(2) Method of collection
The Company don't collect any Personal information items

2. Use of collected information
The Company don't collect any Personal information items

3. Sharing collected information
The Company will not share personal information with a 3rd party

4. Requested Permission
The App can request below permissions.
Camera : It is used only for scan image and recognize barcode. Scanned image will not be saved.
Storage Access : It is used only for export the list of barcode to text file.
```
